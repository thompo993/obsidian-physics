### Experimental Procedure
Tiles were tested on the calibrated rig that had been proven successful on the beamline each tile was repeated and then a tile was chosen to represent a comparison for all other tiles. 
The reason why this is the case as if we were to select an arbitrary number, if the rig changes, comparison is lost for that tile size. 
### 30mm 
the 30mm tile benchmark is tile id21og, as it was verified on the beamline long before my time.  

### 43mm 
the 43mm tiles were also selected of the stave on the beamline, the chosen tile was id43mm 019

### 63mm
After the prd stave testing was complete 63mm tiles were removed from the stave and tested. The resultant selected tile was tile id015.
